Welcome to Pong! 

In this tenis-like game, you take control 
of a vitural paddle and play either the 
computer or a friend in a one on one 
battle to a score of ten!

CONTROLS:

*Left Paddle:
	*Up = W
	*Down = S
*Right Paddle:
	*Up = Up Arrow or Up on Numpad
	*Down = Down Arrow or Down on Numpad

*Pause Game = P or ESC

*Reset Game (When paused or game over) = Space Bar

*Exit Game (When paused or game over) = Q
